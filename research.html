Current Focus

My current focus of research is in non-verbal vocal interaction (NVVI). For example, using pitch or timbre (vowel sounds) as inputs to a controller scheme to control parameters. I hope to publish a literature review I recently wrote and soon perform a study with a prototype NVVI system.
